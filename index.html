What is Software Engineering?

Software engineering is like a jigsaw puzzle to me. You start off by putting together what you know, often the edge pieces. The rest is trying new things and seeing what fits. Sometimes, you see a piece that looks like it works, but later down the line, you realize something else fits perfectly in that slot. I've always loved puzzles. Being able to figure out different ways to solve the problem, organizing and breaking down what I know, and understanding the solution. With each puzzle, I learn different strategies and get better. With this mentality, I want to develop applications, learning more as I solve, and bringing new ideas and creativity to life.
